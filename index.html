My Design Approach

🔍

Research & Empathize

I begin by understanding users, business goals and market context. Through qualitative and quantitative research I uncover pain points and motivations that inform the problem definition.

💡

Ideate & Define

Armed with insights, I brainstorm and define innovative solutions. Sketches, user flows and wireframes help me organise ideas and validate concepts quickly.

🎨

Design & Prototype

I translate concepts into high‑fidelity designs and interactive prototypes using tools like Figma. Systems thinking ensures consistency across components and screens.

🔁

Test & Iterate

Prototypes are validated through usability testing and feedback loops. Insights from testing inform iterative improvements until the design delights users and meets business goals.
